Project Title:
Develop a Platform for Direct Bitcoin Purchases with Visa/MasterCard Payment

Project Description:
I am looking for a developer to create a straightforward platform that enables users to buy Bitcoin directly using Visa or MasterCard, with a small commission charged per transaction. The goal is to provide a simple, fast, and compliant way for users to purchase Bitcoin without requiring them to create an account or hold their funds on the platform. Key details and requirements are as follows:

Key Requirements:
1. Payment Integration:
• Integrate secure payment processing for Visa and MasterCard to facilitate Bitcoin purchases.
2. Bitcoin Purchase and Transfer:
• Upon purchase, the Bitcoin should be sent directly to the buyer’s personal wallet on popular crypto exchanges like Binance or other wallets they specify.
• There should be no need for users to create an account or hold their funds on the platform.
3. Commission Structure:
• Include a small commission on each transaction as a service fee for using the platform.
4. Compliance and Security:
• Since cryptocurrency purchases are restricted, please ensure the platform is designed with maximum security and discretion, keeping all transactions smooth and compliant with international standards.
• The platform should prioritize user privacy and data security.
5. User Interface:
• Simple and intuitive UI focused solely on Bitcoin purchases, making it easy for users to buy and transfer Bitcoin with minimal steps.

Additional Notes:
• I am not looking to add wallets, portfolios, or account management features. The platform should strictly serve as a middleman for buying and transferring Bitcoin.
• I welcome any suggestions on how best to implement this project securely and effectively.

If you have experience with cryptocurrency payment gateways, security, and compliance, please reach out with your proposal and estimated timeline.
