Privacy

This page describes what sdin.dev sends to third parties when you browse the site or use its contact links. It does not cover websites reached through outbound links.

Analytics

The public service pages use Google Analytics to understand aggregate site use. The site can send the current page path, service-page views, mail and phone link clicks, outbound-link destinations, scroll-depth milestones, time-on-page, and a non-monetary lead score.

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar browser storage and process technical information such as device, browser, approximate location, and network data under Google’s terms. sdin.dev does not use Google Signals or ad-personalization signals in its analytics configuration.

The analytics loader does not run when the browser sends Global Privacy Control or a Do Not Track: 1 preference.

Visual assets

Fonts use your device’s system font stack. The animation and icon files used by the interface are packaged with this site, so browsing a service page does not contact a font, icon, or animation CDN.

Contact actions

Choosing an email or telephone link opens the application or provider configured on your device. Any information you then send is handled by that provider and is used to respond to your request. This static site does not host a contact-form database.

Data sharing and retention

sdin.dev does not sell personal information or run advertising profiles. Analytics data is shared with Google as the analytics processor and is retained according to the configured Google Analytics property settings.

Operational logs may also be processed by the static hosting and network providers needed to deliver the site.
