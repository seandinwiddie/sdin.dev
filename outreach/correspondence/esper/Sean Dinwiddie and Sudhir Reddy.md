## Follow-Up Strategy

**Timing**: Wait 3-5 business days after initial outreach

**Hook Options Based on Response Likelihood**:

1. **If no response**:
   * Reference recent Esper announcement or blog post by Sudhir
   * "Your recent article on [edge AI implementation] resonated with a project I just completed..."

2. **If views but no response**:
   * Reference specific technical challenge mentioned in research
   * "I've been thinking about the challenge of managing device diversity at scale since our last exchange..."

3. **If positive response**:
   * Mirror communication style from their response
   * Reference specific points they mentioned
   * Ask follow-up questions about their technical environment
   * Avoid immediate sales pitch

**Tone**: Maintain technical peer-to-peer approach throughout all communications

**Topics to Explore in Conversation**:
- Their approach to managing device diversity challenges
- How they're implementing edge AI capabilities
- Current automation pain points in their development pipeline
- Challenges with scaling engineering talent and operations
