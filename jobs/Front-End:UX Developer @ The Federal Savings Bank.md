Description

The Federal Savings Bank is looking for an experienced front-end developer with a strong application of best-in-class user experience principles. The developer will be part of a team that is building modules designed to support our next-generation initiatives and transform our origination experience.

Ideal candidates are local to the Chicago area and able to work in our Logan Square headquarters 1-2 days a week.

What We Offer

Salary commensurate with experience
Generous vacation days and sick days 
Health, Dental, and Vision insurance
401k with matching
Company-paid Life Insurance
Virtual training modules to broaden your skillsets 
Employee recognition on a national scale
Small bank culture with big bank capabilities

Duties & Responsibilities

Collaborate directly with the Chief Technology Innovation Officer to design and implement web-based applications integrated with our core loan operating system, Encompass
Work with other stakeholders across the organization to discuss the needs, design, and functionality of a website or interface
Collaborate with designers, product managers, and back-end engineers
Create prototypes and mockups of websites or applications
Collaborate with marketing personnel to design graphics and assets suitable for the new application(s)
Integrate multimedia, including audio and video, into the website
Monitor website traffic and deliver reports on traffic metrics

Requirements

Bachelor's degree in a related field (i.e., Computer Science, Web Development, Software Development, etc.)
Experience with scripting languages (PHP, Python, Perl, etc.)
2+ years of JavaScript and jQuery
Experience building web apps
Hands-on experience with JavaScript UI frameworks
Experience using industry-standard version control systems
