About Us

SalesRevv is revolutionizing how membership-based businesses, specifically in the health and wellness sector, manage and grow their sales. Our AI-powered assistants handle everything from converting leads to retaining members, allowing business owners to focus on delivering top-notch services. Business owners can seamlessly monitor activity from our AI-powered assistants and manage their business via our intuitive CRM interface. We solve a major labor force issue by providing small businesses with affordable, high-quality sales talent powered by AI.

SalesRevv was launched in April 2024 and quickly onboarded some of the biggest brands with great initial success. We are now looking to scale on all fronts to meet the market demand and capitalize on this opportunity.
