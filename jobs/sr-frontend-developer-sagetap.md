Job Description

Sagetap is looking for an experienced, curious, collaborative senior frontend developer to join our fully remote engineering team. As a senior member of an engineering- and innovation-first company, you will be empowered to discuss, design, and implement solutions throughout our platform from ideation all the way to production.

An interest in implementing and advocating for good coding practices is important, focusing on writing code that is testable, observable, and maintainable. Ideally, there should be considerations on how systems and code are architected, the selection of optimal tools, and fostering effective collaboration to achieve common objectives.

You will have the opportunity to work with industry standard technologies and drive the adoption of additional tools to build an innovative and evolving product with a team of open and friendly engineers. You will also be given the opportunity to transform this into a full-stack role if you have experience or interest in the back end.

Qualifications

What You’ll Do:

Write code in Typescript, React, and Next.js to develop our front end systems
Communicate clearly with our business and product partners to better understand their requirements, and then help plan, scope, and execute upon them
Be an advocate for best practices around testing, design, observability, etc. to maintain the long-term quality and stability of the platform
Collaborate with, learn from, and teach others on the team about languages, tooling, techniques, etc.
What We're Looking For:

4+ years of software engineering or relevant experience
Proficiency in programming with Typescript, React, and Next.js
Experience with git or other version control systems
Experience in using and helping debug RESTful APIs
An empathetic mindset (for our users and the rest of our team), the ability to collaborate and communicate effectively, and a willingness and desire to learn
Experience in an online marketplace is strongly preferred
