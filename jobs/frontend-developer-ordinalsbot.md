About the job
We are seeking a highly skilled Front-end Developer to join our team at OrdinalsBot, the largest inscription as a service provider in the ordinals space. We’re looking for a seasoned Front-end Developer with a keen eye for design, extensive experience in Next.js, and a passion for creating intuitive user experiences. You’ll be joining a forward-thinking team committed to excellence and eager to implement best practices that resonate throughout the Bitcoin ecosystem.

Responsibilities:

Assist in the development of our cutting-edge web projects, utilizing React and Next.js, creating high-quality, maintainable, and performant code.
Advocate and implement best development practices to ensure excellence in our product offerings.
Help drive the full software development cycle, from conceptualization to deployment and everything in between.
Work collaboratively to craft user-facing features that will be a cornerstone of our customer experience.
Maintain a pulse on emerging trends in the ordinals landscape and the broader scope of software development, applying insights to our technology strategy.
Requirements:

A minimum of 5 years in software development with a proven track record.
In-depth experience with JavaScript, TypeScript, and specifically React/Next.js.
A strong understanding of REST API consumption and integration.
Team collaboration skills to work alongside other developers, designers, and stakeholders.
Exceptional problem-solving abilities.
A perpetual drive to learn and master new technologies and concepts.
Strong communication skills.
Nice to Have:

Proficiency with design tools like Figma and version control using Git.
Expertise in CSS and design systems, creating visually appealing and coherent user interfaces.
Knowledge of ordinals, Bitcoin wallets, and blockchain technology.
Experience with testing patterns to ensure software reliability.
We offer a competitive salary plus benefits. If you are a passionate Front-end Developer and a desire to work on cutting-edge technology, please apply today and join our team at OrdinalsBot!
