About the job
About Bridge

Bridge radically simplifies accepting insurance for telehealth clinics, enabling them to offer patients insurance-covered care within a matter of weeks. By utilizing Bridge, telehealth clinics can tap into nationwide insurance coverage, leverage patient-facing and back-office technology specifically designed for telehealth workflows, and benefit from comprehensive revenue cycle management.

Recently emerging from stealth mode, Bridge has already signed multiple large companies, including public companies. It is led by a team of seasoned healthcare experts with extensive experience across telehealth clinic operations, insurance contracting, and revenue cycle management. Backed by leading investors including General Catalyst, Andreessen Horowitz, Thrive Capital, Khosla Ventures, Greenoaks, and Mischief, we’re poised to scale rapidly.



Role Description

Bridge is seeking a foundational member of our Engineering team. Reporting directly to the CTO, you will play a key role in building and scaling our platform. This position offers the unique opportunity to make a significant impact on our product, working closely with leadership and our clients, shaping the technical foundation of a high-growth company. As a fully remote team member, you’ll have the flexibility to work from anywhere while contributing to a product that improves patient care through technology.



Responsibilities

Full-Stack Development: Design, develop, and maintain scalable web applications using TypeScript, working across the entire stack.
Product Mindset: Understand user needs deeply and contribute to building features that align with customer goals and the broader product vision. Balance technical innovation with delivering real-world value.
System Architecture: Help define and build the architecture of our platform, ensuring it is scalable, robust, and optimized for performance.
Customer Interaction: Engage with customers to gather feedback, translate their needs into technical requirements, and ensure the solutions you build align with our vision and their goals.
Innovation & Improvement: Stay current with industry trends and integrate new technologies that can enhance our product and streamline development processes.


Requirements

7+ years of experience as a full-stack software engineer, with time working in a startup environment
Experience shipping to production with our tech stack - NextJS, SQL, AWS.
Strong problem-solving skills, with a passion for delivering high-quality solutions that align with user needs.
Ability to work independently in a remote environment while being highly communicative and collaborative with a distributed team.
Comfortable working with data, analyzing and improving user experience and product performance.
[Optional] Familiarity with Temporal, or other workflow orchestration frameworks
[Optional] Experience in the healthcare industry, familiarity with HIPAA compliance and handling PHI in a software development environment
